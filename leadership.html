Peter ReynoldsChief Executive Officer

A founder of Avesta, Peter Reynolds participates in all aspects of the company’s strategic planning, investment, and property management activities.

Mr. Reynolds holds a B.A. from Georgetown and a J.D. from Harvard Law School. He is a licensed CPA in Massachusetts, and an attorney and real estate broker in New York and Massachusetts.

Nathaniel FischerChief Financial Officer

A founder of Avesta, Nathaniel Fischer participates in all aspects of the company’s strategic planning, investment, and property management activities.

Mr. Fischer holds a B.A. from Calvin College and a J.D., cum laude, from Harvard Law School. He is an attorney and real estate broker in New York.

Doug MuellerChief Operating Officer

Doug Mueller heads all aspects of Avesta’s property management operations. Mr. Mueller has over 20 years of industry experience in senior leadership roles. Prior to Avesta, he spent 10 years at Sawyer Realty Holdings, based in Maryland. As President of Sawyer, he developed and executed business plans on over 135 communities in 12 states totaling 60,000 units, including a going-private acquisition of Town & Country Trust, a 13,500-unit apartment REIT.

Mr. Mueller also served as Public Affairs Manager in the United States Navy from 1985 to 1995.

Scott BighamSenior Vice President of Construction Services

Scott Bigham oversees construction management, capital planning, and repositioning programs for Avesta and all third party client portfolios. Over the course of his 20-year career in multifamily renovation, Mr. Bigham has successfully completed over 90 major repositioning or condo conversion projects comprising 20,000 units and $100,000,000 total spend. Prior to Avesta, he served as Vice President of Construction Management for the Pinnacle Companies.

Mr. Bigham holds a B.A. from University of Central Florida and is a licensed real estate broker in Florida.

Eric ReskerSenior Vice President of Financial Operations

Eric Resker maintains full responsibility for all aspects of financial stewardship and executive leadership for property management operations. In that role, Mr. Resker executes asset management, accounting, budgeting, financial analysis, and internal control evaluation. He also leads revenue management, assessing market conditions and property performance to optimize product pricing. Prior to Avesta, Mr. Resker was SVP of Financial Operations and Revenue management for AIMCO. He has also held senior operational and financial roles with companies in commercial real estate development, metals and mining, and manufacturing.

Mr. Resker holds a B.A. from Allegheny College and an M.B.A. from Fordham University.

Robert ReynoldsVice President of Operations

Robert Reynolds is an experienced real estate investor and manager with over 7 years of multifamily experience. As Vice President of Operations, Mr. Reynolds oversees daily property management operations across Avesta’s entire portfolio. Prior to Avesta, he was involved in the acquisition and management of 25 multifamily properties. He has also worked as a chemistry and biology teacher in New York.

Mr. Reynolds holds a B.A. from Franciscan University of Steubenville, an M.S. from Mercy College, and an M.S. from NYIT.

Nick WilliamsVice President of Maintenance

Nick Williams spearheads maintenance operations at Avesta, overseeing maintenance needs for all of Avesta’s properties. Mr. Williams has over 20 years of experience in multifamily maintenance. Prior to joining Avesta, he spent 16 years at Post Properties where he was most recently a regional maintenance supervising engineer. While at Post, he was awarded Engineer of the year amongst many other Bay Area recognitions.

Mr. Williams hold certifications in AC&R Safety Coalition, Pool Operator, Universal Refrigerant Transition and Recovery.
